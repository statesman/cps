Child abuse and neglect fatality database

In 2009, the Texas legislature ordered Child Protective Services to publicly record every abuse- and neglect-related death in the state. The Statesman obtained the reports using the Texas Public Information Act and extracted important data points to gain insight into the almost 800 deaths detailed in the reports. Those data points and the associated analysis, partially represented below, represents the only comprehensive review of the reports since the state was required in 2009 by lawmakers to generate them.
